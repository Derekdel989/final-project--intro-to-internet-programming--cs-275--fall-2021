In fact, it's easy to take high doses of them because their active ingredients-the antihistamines diphenhydramine and doxylamine-aren't only in OTC sleep products such as Sominex and ZzzQuil. They're also in nighttime pain rellievers such as Advil PM and Tylenol pm, as well as allergy meds such as Benadryl.
